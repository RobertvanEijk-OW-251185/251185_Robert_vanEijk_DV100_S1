ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Red Planet Ventures began as a passion project among a group of aerospace engineers, entrepreneurs, and dreamers who shared one vision: making space travel a reality for everyday people. Founded in 2098, the company launched its first successful commercial flight to Mars just two years later—marking a new era in interplanetary exploration. Since then, we’ve expanded our routes to include destinations like the Moon, Venus, and Jupiter’s moons, becoming the first space tourism company to offer regular voyages across the solar system. What started as a single launch has evolved into a fleet of state-of-the-art spacecraft and a global community of space explorers.p>

OUR MISSION

At Red Planet Ventures, our mission is to make space accessible, inspiring, and unforgettable. We strive to lead the way in safe, sustainable interplanetary travel, connecting people with the vast beauty and mystery of our solar system. By combining advanced aerospace technology with world-class hospitality, we’re committed to delivering life-changing journeys that push the boundaries of what’s possible—one planet at a time.
